College Facility

The College has Special Study Centres/Distance Education of IGNOU and NSOU which offer professional & job-oriented programmes along with conventional courses. Rain water harvesting project has been started with the financial help from Environment Science Departments, Govt. of West Bengal to meet the need for water to be used by the students of Swabalambi hostel. There are four electrical generator sets in the campus if emergencies arise. Selected students are provided with sports goods for participation in Inter-University and State Level competitions. The college has a well-equipped gymnasium-hall and ‘Annasatra’ for meager students. Very recent five PG courses in science and Humanities introduced in our college is running smoothly.

Information

It fulfils the academic need of the students and nourishes them with the knowledge of real value. The college was initially affiliated to the University of Calcutta and later on to the University of Burdwan and now under the affiliation of the Bankura University. The college is recognized by the UGC under 2f and 12B. The college, with best academic standard, offers twenty Honours & Programme at UG level and five courses at P.G. level. The rate of success in the University Final Examination is more than 90%. Along with this excellent academic performance the students are involved in social activities like Extension Programme, Adult Education Programme, organizing Health Camp, Plantation Programme, etc. With equal enthusiasm the students serve the nation by being an integral part of N.S.S. & N.C.C. units of the college.
